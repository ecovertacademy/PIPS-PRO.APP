What is PIPS Pro?

PIPS Pro™ (Property Inspection Profit System) is a comprehensive property assessment platform designed for property investors, surveyors, and inspection professionals. It provides 38 structured protocols across 5 tiers for evaluating properties using a rating‑based methodology (0‑10 scale).

The platform includes photo annotation with drawing tools, automated PDF report generation, and full assessment history. Unlike simple checklists, PIPS Pro™ gives you nuanced, data‑driven insights for better investment decisions.
